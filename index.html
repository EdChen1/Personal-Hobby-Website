Lyon

The sickly but beloved prince of the Grado Empire.

Lyon's first and only apperance was in Fire Emblem: The Sarced Stones. Born very weak physically, Lyon was a great scholar in the magical arts and was extremly loved by the people. Unfortunately, after performing a selfless dark ritual to attempt to save his country, he became possessed by a demon spirit.

Shannan

The direct inheritor of a legendary weapon.

Shannan's first apperance was in Fire Emblem: Genealogy of the Holy War. A direct descendant from the Twelve Crusaders, he possesses great strength and magical fortitude. It is rumered he personally defeated five elite generals by himself.
