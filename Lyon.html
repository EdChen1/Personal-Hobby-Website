Stat Distrubition

This page serves to show this unit's specfic stat distrubiton.

Each unit has a asset and a flaw, which increases the affected stat by a +3, or a -3, respectively. Obtaining a certain asset/flaw combination greatly affects the unit's performance to the point of either a powerhouse or barely unusable.

Optimal skills loadout:

  • A Slot: Triangle Adept 3
  • B Slot: Sword/Tome-breaker 3
  • C Slot: Attack Smoke 3
  • S Slot: Quick Risposte 3

Assets

  • +ATK: Any offensive unit worth thier salt will enjoy a attack asset, leading to more damage and overall better perfomance.
  • +DEF: Thanks to Lyon's already servicable base Defense, a further increase in either of these two are also acceptable.

Flaws

  • -SPD: Due to the fact that Lyon can fight at a advantage against two different types of units as well as great defenses, taking a speed flaw is optimal.

Summary

Lyon possesses an Attack stat of 34 which is very good for a red mage. Combined with his personal weapon Naglfar, Lyon is able to deal a good amount of damage in a single attack. Lyon also possesses good mixed bulk with 41 HP, 26 Defense and 30 Resistance which works well with Naglfar which is effectively a more powerful raven tome, granting Lyon with a weapon triangle advantage against colorless units. This does come at the cost of pitiful Speed however at just 21, meaning Lyon is very liable to receiving double attacks.
