Stat Distrubition

This page serves to show this unit's specfic stat distrubiton.

Each unit has a asset and a flaw, which increases the affected stat by a +3, or a -3, respectively. Obtaining a certain asset/flaw combination greatly affects the unit's performance to the point of either a powerhouse or barely unusable.

Assets/Flaws HP Strength Speed Defense Resistence

+ 46 42 39 40 21

Optimal skills loadout:

  • A Slot: Kestral Stance/Distant Counter
  • B Slot: Repel 3 or Close Call 3
  • C Slot: Pulse Smoke 3
  • S Slot: Swift Stance 2

Assets

  • +ATK: benefits immensely from receiving a +ATK to further build upon its strong base Attack and enhance his raw damage output; it even comes as a four-point Asset to boot.
  • +SPD: Increasing his Speed with a +SPD is another valid choice to enhance his ability to consistently perform and deny follow-up attacks

Flaws

  • -HP: Shannan can safely take a -HP without much detriment to his overall performance, making it his ideal choice of Flaw. Furthermore, it pairs quite nicely with skills that are dependent on maintaining low levels of HP.
  • -RES: Magic users are Shannan's kyrtonite, with his extraordinary low resistence. Losing 3 more points in this catergoty does not hurt him.

Summary

Shannan was game changing when he entered the game. His weapon along with his through the roof Speed and Attack stats make him a untouchable unit. Furthermore, with his abnormally high Defense and better than average HP, he hits hard and takes a lot of punches before going down. Best paired with healers and units that increases stats.
